Chicken Tikka Masala

Below are the instructions on how to make Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

    Marinate

    1 cup of yogurt
    1 tbsp of lemon juice
    2 tsp of fresh ground cumin
    1 tsp of ground cinnamon
    2 tsp of cayenne pepper
    2 tsp of freshly ground black pepper
    1 tbsp of minced fresh ginger
    1 tsp of salt or to taste
    3 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
    4 long skewers

    Sauce

    2 tbsp of butter
    3 clove garlic, minced
    1 jalapeno pepper, finely chopped
    2 tsp of ground cumin
    2 tsp of paprika
    1 tsp of salt or to taste
    1 (15 ounce) can tomato Sauce
    1.75 cups of heavy cream
    1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro

Step 1 - Marinate

    Melt butter in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Sauté garlic and jalapeno for 1 minute.

Step 2 - Grill

    Preheat a grill for high heat. Lightly oil the grill grate. Thread chicken onto skewers, and discard marinade. Grill until juices run clear, about 5 minutes on each side

Step 3 - Sauce

    Melt butter in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Sauté garlic and jalapeno for 1 minute. Season with 2 tsp of cumin, 2 tsp of paprika, 3 tsp of Garam Masala & 1/2 tsp of salt. Stir in 15 oz. can of tomato sauce and about 1.75 cups of heavy cream. Simmer on low heat until sauce thickens for about 20 mins. Add grilled chicken and simmer for 10 minutes. Transfer to a serving platter and garnish with fresh cilantro.
